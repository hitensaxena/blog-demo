The Journey Inward

  • Immersion

    Māyā’s tunnel vision tightens; the story feels absolute and the play is all-consuming.

  • Insight

    A crack appears; the tunnel widens, light pours in and context returns. The illusion begins to thin.

  • Integration

    Lessons logged, behaviours tuned, gratitude recorded. Each cycle shortens the stay in illusion.

  • Base Reality

    The journey returns to its source: the witnessing awareness that remains untouched, present before, during and after every story.
